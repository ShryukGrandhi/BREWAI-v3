# Charcoal Eats US - Operations Manual

## Overview
Charcoal Eats US is a fast-casual wing restaurant located at 370 Lexington Avenue, New York, NY. We specialize in made-to-order chicken wings with creative sauces and sides.

**Operating Hours:**
- Monday - Thursday: 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM
- Friday - Saturday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- Sunday: 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM

## Staffing Structure

### Roles
1. **General Manager** - Overall operations, scheduling, inventory, P&L
2. **Shift Manager** - Floor supervision, customer service, quality control
3. **Cook** - Wing preparation, frying, saucing, quality standards
4. **Prep Cook** - Morning prep, inventory receiving, cleaning
5. **Cashier/Server** - Order taking, POS, customer service
6. **Delivery Driver** - Order delivery, customer interaction (contracted via DoorDash/Uber)

### Staffing Guidelines
- **Capacity**: Each cook can handle 25 orders per hour at peak efficiency
- **Minimum Staff**: 1 cook + 1 cashier during slow periods
- **Peak Staff**: 3 cooks + 2 cashiers during lunch/dinner rush
- **Manager Coverage**: Manager on duty during all operating hours

## Daily Operations

### Opening Procedures (10:00 AM)
1. Unlock and disarm security system
2. Turn on all kitchen equipment (fryers, warmers, steam table)
3. Check refrigeration temperatures (log in book)
4. Verify cash drawer ($200 starting cash)
5. Check online ordering systems (DoorDash, UberEats, website)
6. Complete morning prep (see prep.md)
7. Staff briefing and day's goals

### Service Operations
1. **Order Taking**
   - Greet customers within 30 seconds
   - Suggest combos or add-ons
   - Confirm order before sending to kitchen
   - Provide estimated wait time (8-12 minutes for wings)

2. **Kitchen Flow**
   - Orders print at wing station
   - Cook calls out ticket times
   - Expeditor checks quality before handoff
   - All orders garnished with celery/carrots and dressing

3. **Quality Standards**
   - Wings must be crispy, fully coated in sauce
   - Internal temp 165°F minimum
   - No burnt or undercooked items
   - Proper portion sizes (10 wings = 10 wings, not 9 or 11)

### Closing Procedures (10:00 PM weekdays, 11:00 PM weekends)
1. Lock front door, flip sign to "Closed"
2. Stop accepting new orders 15 minutes before close
3. Complete final orders
4. Clean and filter fryers
5. Sanitize all surfaces
6. Stock and cover prep for next day
7. Take trash to dumpster
8. Count cash drawer and complete deposit
9. Arm security system and lock up

## Customer Service Standards

### Core Principles
- **Fast**: 8-12 minute ticket times
- **Friendly**: Smile, make eye contact, use names
- **Fresh**: Made-to-order, hot food
- **Flexible**: Accommodate reasonable requests

### Handling Complaints
1. **Listen actively** - Let customer explain fully
2. **Apologize sincerely** - Even if not our fault
3. **Solve immediately** - Remake order or offer refund
4. **Follow up** - Ensure customer is satisfied before leaving
5. **Document** - Log complaint in system for trends

### Busy Period Management
- Call out wait times clearly
- Offer water or samples during waits
- Keep lobby clean and organized
- Update online ordering times when backed up

## Health & Safety

### Food Safety
- **Handwashing**: Every 30 minutes, after handling raw food, after touching face/hair
- **Glove use**: Required for handling ready-to-eat food
- **Temperature danger zone**: Keep food out of 40°F - 140°F
- **Cross contamination**: Separate cutting boards for raw meat and vegetables

### Allergens
- **Common allergens**: Wheat (breading), dairy (sauces), soy (marinades)
- **Disclosure**: Always inform customers of ingredients
- **Gluten-free option**: Grilled wings available (no breading)

### Cleaning Schedule
- **Hourly**: Wipe counters, sweep floors, check restrooms
- **Daily**: Deep clean fryers, sanitize all surfaces, mop floors
- **Weekly**: Clean equipment (under fryers, behind line), hood cleaning
- **Monthly**: Full deep clean, equipment maintenance

## Financial Metrics

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- **Food Cost %**: Target 28-32% of sales
- **Labor Cost %**: Target 25-30% of sales
- **Average Order Value**: $18-22
- **Customer Frequency**: 2-3 visits per month (regulars)

### Sales Patterns
- **Peak hours**: 12-2 PM (lunch), 6-8 PM (dinner)
- **Peak days**: Friday and Saturday
- **Slow periods**: Monday/Tuesday evenings, 2-5 PM daily

### Budget Authority
- **Shift Manager**: Up to $50 for customer resolutions
- **General Manager**: Up to $500 for operational needs
- **Owner approval**: Required for purchases over $500
